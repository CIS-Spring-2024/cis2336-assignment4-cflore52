Welcome to Camila's Biography Page!

Get to know the owner of The Study Cafe!






Camila Flores is the proud owner of the Study Cafe. With over 10 years of experience in management, Camila wanted to open a Cafeteria on the UH Sugar Land Campus, that would allow students to use their Cougar Cash and Shasta Bucks.


Camila came across the idea of opening a Cafeteria on the Sugar Land campus, when she realized she was spending a lot of time trying to find places to eat around campus. Not only was it time consuming, but also expensive. Camila decided that opening a cafeteria with various lunch options would be convenient to the students, and the school, as it would provide another source of income.


In addition to opening the Study Cafe and managing it, Camila, an 18 year old, is a current UH Computer Information Systems student and is set to graduate May of 2025. She has an internship at a non-profit, that has taught her much of what she knows about business management now.


When she is not at school, work, or managing the Study Cafe, Camila enjoys running, hiking, reading, and shopping.
